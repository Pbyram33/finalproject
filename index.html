Demon Slayer

Summary

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive - Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister. He meets many friends and enemies along the way and is challenged to defeat challenging demons while trying to find a cure for his sister.
